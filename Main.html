Requirements

Educational & Professional Requirements

Bachelor’s degree (often in Education, English, or related field). TEFL/TESOL/CELTA certification (for teaching English as a foreign/second language). Strong English proficiency (speaking, reading, writing, grammar).

Technical / Digital Skills

Knowledge of online teaching platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, MS Teams). Ability to use digital tools (interactive whiteboards, PowerPoint, Google Classroom, Kahoot, Quizlet). Basic troubleshooting (internet, audio, and video setup).

Equipment Requirements (if teaching online)

Stable high-speed internet. Computer/laptop with updated software. Good quality headset and microphone. Webcam with clear resolution.

Teaching & Classroom Skills

Lesson planning and curriculum design. Classroom management. Assessment and evaluation. Ability to adapt to different learning styles.
